What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

App Permission Details
To provide our services, we may request the following permissions:

Internet & ACCESS_WIFI_STATE
INTERNET permission allows the app to access the internet to retrieve and display ads. We do not collect personal information through this permission. And ACCESS_WIFI_STATE permission allows the app to determine the state of Wi-Fi connectivity. This helps optimize ad delivery based on the user's network conditions. We do not access or store any personal data related to your Wi-Fi connections.
 

Read Write External Storage Permission:
Read External Storage permission allows the app to access files on your device. This is necessary for users to select files that they want to include in their invoices. Write External Storage permission enables the app to save the generated invoices to your device. This ensures that you can easily access and manage your invoices at any time.

SMS Permission:
READ_SMS permission allows the app to access your SMS messages. We use this access to retrieve bank transaction details, enabling us to present accurate and up-to-date information about your financial activities. RECEIVE_SMS permission allows the app to receive SMS messages, which may include important transaction alerts and notifications. This helps us keep you informed about your banking activities in real time.

 

We may collect the data from these messages to further enhance our app's features and improve your experience. Please be assured that we are committed to protecting your privacy and will not share your SMS and data with third parties.
